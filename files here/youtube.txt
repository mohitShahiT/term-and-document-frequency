YouTube is an American online video sharing and social media platform launched 
by Steve Chen, Chad Hurley, and Jawed Karim in February 2005. 
After Google, YouTube is the most visited website worldwide, with over one 
billion monthly users.[7] Its users watch more than one billion hours of videos each day,
[8] and, As of May 2019, it was estimated that videos were being uploaded at a rate of 
more than 500 hours of content per minute.
